About Me

Hi! I'm Chloe

I'm a Human Factors Engineering and Computer Science student at Tufts University, passionate about designing systems that are as intuitive as they are impactful.

Currently based in Boston and Hong Kong, I focus on human-centered design, digital accessibility, and emerging tech. Whether I'm wireframing in Figma, analyzing user behavior, or building solutions in code, I aim to create experiences that feel thoughtful, inclusive, and beautifully simple.

I love projects that bridge systems thinking with storytelling—designing not just for how things work, but for how people feel using them.
